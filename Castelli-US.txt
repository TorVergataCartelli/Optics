User Stories:

1) As a softair player, I want to find playgrounds and read information about them, 
   so that I can choose where to go.

2) As a playground owner, I want to show to other users information about my playground, 
   so that players could know it and come to play.

3) As a softair player, I want to review a playground where I've been, 
   so that I can give my opinion and help other users deciding the playground.
